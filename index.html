Speak More

Nervous to talk?

  1. Run a traning lesson on something you are already good at and ship it around to all the meetups - I promise you, they need speakers. Intro nights are helpful for beginners - get started by running an "Hello World" for the language you know best and offer to give it at a meetup.

  2. Tech or TA at a local bootcamp. You don't have to be an expert, many bootcampers are beginners. You can make a little side $$ AND you get comfortable speaking every week in front of a crowd. Many bootcamps will also offer one off classes on more advanced subjects. Check out General Assembly, 1776, I/O Spaces, the Arlington Public Library, ByteBack, and the new GW Bootcamp.
